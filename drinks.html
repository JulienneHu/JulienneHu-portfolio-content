Cold Fruit Green Tea

5 mins

1. Brew tea with a little hot water to stimulate the aroma of the tea, then add water and crushed fruit you like (such as peaches.
2. Put it in the refrigerator for more than 12 hours.
3. Pick a beautiful glass and add ice cubes to enjoy!

Lemon Sparkling Water

5 mins

Chop the lemons and limes, then add the honey, sparkling water, and ice cubes and mix well. Wait five minutes for the flavors to blend and enjoy!
