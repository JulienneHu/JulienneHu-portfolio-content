Blueberry Bagel

10 mins

Blend greek yogurt with oreo and berry jelly, then carefully whip it on the bagel. This blueberry jelly is sugar-free also you can make it with frozen strawberries, blueberries and blackberries with sugar substitute!

Potato Cheese Sandwich

20 mins

Add the mashed potatoes, ham, corn kernels and chopped cucumber to the milk, salad dressing and some salt and black pepper and stir well. Spread them on toasted brioche toast with a slice of cheese. Finally toast the sandwiches to melt the cheese and enjoy breakfast.
