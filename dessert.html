Fruit Cake

180 mins

1. Crush the digestive biscuits, pour them into the mold, add the melted butter, mix well, compact, and refrigerate.
2. Whip the cream with a little white sugar until there are textures but disappear immediately. Pour the squeezed fruit milkshake into it and stir well, then melt the gelatin powder with water and pour it into the cream.
3. Take out the mold from the refrigerator, pour the whipped cream into the mold, put a little fruit fruit when halfway in, and then pour in the rest of the cream.
4. Arrange the washed and cut fruits on the top according to the shape you like, and then put them in the refrigerator for at least four hours.
5. Take out the cake and use a hot towel around the outside of the mold to make it easier to demould.
6. Decorate.

Cranberry Scone

30 mins

1. Combine low-gluten flour, baking powder, icing sugar and sea salt.
2. Add cranberries, butter, eggs and milk and mix well.
3. Let stand for one hour.
4. Press the mold into the desired shape, and then brush the surface with egg wash.
5. Bake for twenty minutes.
