Cold Brew

5 mins

1. Use a little hot water to brew coffee powder to stimulate the aroma, then add water.
2. Put it in the refrigerator for more than 12 hours.
3. Pick a beautiful glass and add ice cubes to enjoy!
Tips: Fruit sparkling water and cold brew are a perfect match!

Mocha

10 mins

1. Use a moka pot to extract the coffee liquid.
2. Add milk and half&half to the coffee liquid and you will get a delicious milk coffee.
Tips: flavored ground coffee is better! (such as caramel ground coffee, hazelnut ground coffee)
